All About Prehistory

Why I'm Interested

My whole life I’ve just had a sort of passion for prehistory and the ancient world, predominantly the world of dinosaurs. It may have come from my brother’s appreciation for Jurassic Park and his collection of dinosaur legos, or maybe the lesson on dinosaurs and fossils my class was taught in second grade. Either way, I’ve had an undying love for the terrible lizards that once roamed the Earth.
